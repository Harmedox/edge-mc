Join the KCP-Edge community

KCP-Edge is an open source project that anyone in the community can use, improve, and enjoy. We'd love you to join us! Here's a few ways to find out what's happening and get involved.

Learn and Connect

Using or want to use KCP-Edge? Find out more here:

  • User mailing list: Discussion and help from your fellow users
  • YouTube Channel: View recordings of past KCP-Edge community meetings
  • Medium Blog Series: Follow us on Medium to read about community developments
  • LinkedIn: See what others are saying about the community

Develop and Contribute

If you want to get more involved by contributing to KCP-Edge, join us here:

  • GitHub: Development takes place here!
  • Slack: Chat with other project developers
  • Developer mailing list: Discuss development issues around the project

You can find out how to contribute to KCP-Edge in our Contribution Guidelines
